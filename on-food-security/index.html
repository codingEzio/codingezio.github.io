On Food Security

Posted on October 28, 2022

Source: Food Security - A Growing Dilemma | DW Documentary

Climate change affects everything, including the one we are so depended on, Food. As you all know, about half (reference needed) the carbon emission is produced during the food producton (it's just one part of the giant framework, like transportation would also be included).

Different people face different kinds of problems.

Non-native

For farmers, more diverse kinds of food (down to seed), including importing food which has never been planted in the local area or even country. To main thing is just to increase the robustness, and to invest for the the future, I did not use prepare as it might be just the reason why the food isn't produced in this country.

Also, simply using it might be free from the pests, as the pests need to be adaptive to attack newly introduced one as well 😂

Sea

Food in sea: sea weed. Healthier (vegetables in general are for that though) and more diverse (meat + veggie || meat + veggie from sea). Also, it develops faster (based on the researched have been made) and is able to reduce carbon emmissions (win-win, ay?).

Not general enough, as it might be more diverted into other purposes like bio-fuel instead of food.

Not getting enough supervison as there were not enough researchers or professional looking over it.

Little Summary

It's definitely not a easy task, whether it's planting non-native speicies or food in the sea (like sea weed).
