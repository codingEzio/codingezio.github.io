On Anxiety towards AI

Posted on December 06, 2022

Frustration

From AIs like Github Copilot that could helps you write code, to something like ChatGPT which could also answer questions from quite a wide range of fields. Are they fun to play with? Hell yes. But, deep down, I'm not really too passionate about it.

  • .. If it can do it, why should I learn any new things any more?
  • .. If it know about the concepts, why should I learn?
  • .. If it could write the whole app, why should I exist 😂
  • ..

But

I am

  • being irrational
  • being too anxious
  • being arrogant about Human qualities

I shall

  • calm my mind down
  • ask good questions to get the good out of the AIs
  • prepare for the next one that's even more powerful (GPT-4)

That's it for now.
