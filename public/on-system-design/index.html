On System Design

Posted on December 04, 2022

Intro

Why

As you and I all know, even for a normal Spring project, it's already complex enough for the human brain to comprehend just in one go (with or without a good documentation). Just like it would be unimaginably more complex for projects like Google Play Store that would need to provide various APIs, payment stuff and many other details (I was going to choose Twitter back then, then I realized that it wasn't really looking that complicated for a non-technical person. Anyway, just think of it as a really complicated project, and a growing one!).

For me, I think the reason for companies like Google asking interview questions that related to System Design, is to see if the person really has the ability to have a clear grasp and an organized view for the projects she/he/x gonna built, and also, you would need to anticipate for the future. As the project grows, you have to consider scaling resources like Database and concepts like Database Sharding.
