On Ecosystem Simulation

Posted on December 05, 2022

Why

I'm always quite fascinated by the idea of Food Chain which is to achieve a Balance of Nature. But how? Really?

I just could not visualize those things in my head. Take a dumb example I just came out of, suppose the predators hunted all their preys before the preys could reproduce enough to avoid a extirpation (or Local Extinction), and having two constraints that the predators won't migrate out, and no preys of theirs will enter in this specific area.

I wanted a tool that could help me do that! At least to know the basics to start going :)

Note

I have little to none knowledge on this subject. Currently I'm still researching on the materials to read and watch on :)
