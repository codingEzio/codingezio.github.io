On Prepare for the Inevitable

Posted on January 29, 2023

Do these as soon as your parents reach 40 or so. This post would be regularly updated to remind me to be grateful.

Issue

  • We are constantly influenced by our emotions to love properly
  • We are too impatient caused by various things
  • We won't notice it until it's too late

Solution-ish

一

  • Do NOT WAIT

二

  • Embrace the hassle of teaching them how things work
  • Embrace their world views, truly
  • Embrace non-critical rumour
  • Photograph them w/wo you in the frame when going out

三

  • Regular compulsory high-standard health check-up (/yr)
  • Regular direct communication by phone
  • Embrace moderate smoking
  • Embrace moderate drinking

四

  • Check and learn about the insurance your family got
  • Let them have a sense of participation in your decision
  • No cursing in whichever discussion/quarrel you're in
