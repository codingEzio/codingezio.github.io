### Why

> To re-live the wonders that once seemed mundance has always been fun, like when people for the first time knew about the existence of microorganisms in the 17th century. There might some people once guesses about it, fantasied them. But it's nothing not before people actually to *see* them *in person*, how amazing is that!
>
> And, for *scale*, the [spatial scale](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spatial_scale) to be exact, you won't be amazed unless you actually trying to think it through and trying to visualize them.
>> The ant is *small*, but **how small**? The [volume ratio](https://old.reddit.com/r/FilmTheorists/comments/q29xlj/how_many_ants_would_it_actually_take_to_make_one/) for the human-to-ant is roughly [millions](https://www.answers.com/zoology/How_many_ants_would_fit_in_a_human_body). But what is *millions*, exactly? Or any other scales?
