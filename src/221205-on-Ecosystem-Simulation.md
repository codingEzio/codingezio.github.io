## Why

> I'm always quite fascinated by the idea of [*Food Chain*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Food_chain) which is to achieve a [Balance of Nature](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Balance_of_nature). But how? Really?
>
> I just could not visualize those things in my head. Take a dumb example I just came out of, suppose *the predators hunted all their preys* **before** *the preys could reproduce enough* to avoid a **extirpation** <small>(or [*Local Extinction*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Local_extinction))</small>, and having two constraints that *the predators won't migrate out*, and *no preys of theirs will enter in this specific area*.
>
> I wanted a tool that could help me do that! At least to know the basics to start going :)

## Note

> I have little to none knowledge on this subject. Currently I'm still researching on the materials to read and watch on :)
