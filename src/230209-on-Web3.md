## Prologue

> It wasn't clear to me about what the technologies brought by [*Web3*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Web3) could do anything until I noticed the [*Smart Contract*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Smart_contract) concept along with it. It was exactly the reason why we [*codify* so many things](https://codingezio.github.io/230124-on-Everything-as-Code.html) like *Infrastructure as Code* <small>(like [*Terraform*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terraform_(software)))</small> , *Diagram as Code* <small>(like [*Mermaid*](https://mermaid.js.org/intro/))</small>. For *Web3*, it codify the *real-world contract* <small>(in a much more detailed and robust way, conceptually)</small>. The sooner we codify most of the things in the business process, the sooner we could see why it's a great that could benefit humans a lot.
>
> But the future might not come, it's not as unstoppable as the [AGI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artificial_general_intelligence) in my humble opinion.
