### Notes

1. Find the *recipe* <small>(what you wanna eat)</small>
2. Extract the *steps*
3. Prepare the appropriate *pots*
4. Prepare the appropriate *plates*
5. Prepare the *ingredients*
6. Confirm the exact amount of *spice* you'd need
7. Get utiliies that would ease the mundane steps like *切片*
