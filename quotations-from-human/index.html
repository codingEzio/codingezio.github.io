Quotations from Human

Posted on December 09, 2022

As you can see from my posts, almost all of them were trying to figure something out, or to find something that I could make use of. But that's not how the life goes, sometimes it's best to just not having any goals in mind. Sorrows like losing your relatives, there were no medications to resolve that.

This post is merely trying to capture the beauty produced by our fellow humans.

Sleeping At Last - "Saturn" (Official Music Video)

I lost my brother my dad and my sister some years back and everyday I come back to this beautiful song... life can be difficult and cruel at times and sooner or later we lose someone we love,but never give up because life can also be beautiful.. "how rare and beautiful it is to even exist"

My psychiatrist introduced me to this song after my suicide attempt. I come back to this song, everytime i have the feeling of a growing dark hole inside of me, so i can see the light again.

I came back after my loved ones died. I came back after i had to quit my dream job.
I came back after my miscarriages.

And everytime i come back to this song to understand that the universe is made to be seen by my eyes. Thats why i'm still here.

You know what I realized? All of the comments on Sleeping at Last songs are supportive, genuine, and kind. There are no swear words, hateful remarks, or harsh feelings. Everyone is at peace and wants to be gentle. I feel that this is strongly because of the type of music Sleeping at Last is. Heartfelt, calm, and sincere.

Man, I listen to this song almost every day, especially when I'm close to falling asleep. and it makes me cry like a child. sometimes when I listen to her makes me wonder how shitty my life is. I'm dying and no one is noticing.
