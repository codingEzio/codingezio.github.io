# Things Change Instantly Like a Lightning

### Like Who's Last Day?

> People often give advices like "*treat this like this is your last day of your life*", "*time is precious, we must make use of the every last second we have*". Well, most of them had failed to mention something that is outside the context of *YOU*.
>
> What if tomorrow is the *last day of your dad's life*? or *your mon's life*, *your grandparent's life*?
>> You gotta be in this exact situation to feel that level of intense emotion :|
